Why we verify every review

To protect venues from bad-faith reviews and ensure artists can trust what they read, every review is manually checked before publishing. Accepted proof includes:

  • A link to a public event listing (Bandsintown, Songkick, Facebook Events, Eventbrite)
  • A Setlist.fm entry for the show
  • A photo of the contract, rider, or settlement sheet (personal details can be redacted)
  • A ticket stub, poster, or advance screenshot featuring your name
  • A venue-provided booking confirmation email

What gets rated

We ask about the things that actually matter: Sound & PA quality, Load-in & backline, Green room & hospitality, Promoter communication, Door deal & pay accuracy, and an overall "Would play again?" score.

Who can submit

Any performing artist who has played a ticketed or publicly listed show at the venue — solo artists, bands, DJs, comedians, and spoken word performers alike.
